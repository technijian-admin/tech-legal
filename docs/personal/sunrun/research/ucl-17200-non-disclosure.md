## Statute
§ 17200 prohibits "any unlawful, unfair or fraudulent business act or practice." Three independent prongs.

### "Unlawful" — Borrows from Any Other Law
Predicates: CLRA § 1770(a)(5)/(a)(14); Civ. Code §§ 1572, 1710; Bus. & Prof. § 7169 (CSLB Solar Disclosure); Bus. & Prof. § 7159.5; breach of PPA § 8. *Cel-Tech Communications v. L.A. Cellular* (1999) 20 Cal.4th 163, 180.

### "Unfair" — Three Tests, All Reach Sunrun
1. *Cel-Tech* "tethered" test — practice violates spirit of antitrust/CP laws.
2. Balancing test (*Klein v. Chevron* (2012) 202 Cal.App.4th 1342, 1376) — Sunrun's utility (avoid truck-roll cost) dwarfed by $2,583 SDG&E loss + $2,500 projected NEM true-up.
3. FTC test — substantial injury, not outweighed, not reasonably avoidable. Homeowner could not avoid because Sunrun withheld the precise fact (zero output) needed for self-help.

### "Fraudulent" — Likely-to-Deceive
No scienter required. *Comm. on Children's TV v. Gen. Foods* (1983) 35 Cal.3d 197, 211. Material omissions actionable on duty or partial-rep theory. *In re Tobacco II Cases* (2009) 46 Cal.4th 298, 327–28.

## Standing — Prop 64
Must have "suffered injury in fact and lost money or property." (§ 17204.) Homeowner's $2,583 documented SDG&E excess satisfies *Kwikset Corp. v. Sup. Ct.* (2011) 51 Cal.4th 310, 322–23.

## Remedies
- **Restitution** (§ 17203) — disgorge autopay PPA payments made during zero-production months.
- **Injunction** — repair, accurate fault notices within 48 hrs of detection, honor EPG.
- **No damages** under UCL (*Korea Supply v. Lockheed* (2003) 29 Cal.4th 1134, 1144) — pair with CLRA/concealment for compensatory + punitives.
- **C.C.P. § 1021.5** private attorney general fees if action confers public benefit (enjoining vague-disclosure pattern statewide).

## Critical Case
***McGill v. Citibank*** (2017) 2 Cal.5th 945, 961 — arbitration provisions waiving the right to seek **public injunctive relief** in any forum are unenforceable. Directly applies because Sunrun's vague "system issue" practice is generalized to its CA customer base.

## Risks
- Restitution limited to money/property defendant has — third-party SDG&E loss = damages (CLRA), not UCL restitution. *Korea Supply*.
- 4-yr SOL (§ 17208); discovery rule applies (*Aryeh v. Canon* (2013) 55 Cal.4th 1185, 1192).
