## Coverage
CLRA reaches "transaction[s] … intended to result in the sale or lease of goods or services to any consumer" (§ 1761(d), (e)). California prosecutors apply CLRA to residential solar PPAs (Vivint Solar, Riverside DA 2021 $4.3M; San Diego DA Feb 19, 2026).

## § 1770(a)(14) — Misrepresenting Rights/Remedies
Prohibits representing "that a transaction confers or involves rights, remedies, or obligations that it does not have or involve, or that are prohibited by law."

**Application:** Sunrun rep Katherine Wilson wrote 4/17/2026 "you do not have a performance guarantee on your contract." That misrepresents an existing contractual right — 2012 PPA § 8 ("Guaranteed Output and Refunds") *is* a written production guarantee with annual refund mechanism, *and* an Early Performance Guarantee email auto-issued from Sunrun on 4/16/2026. Stripping that remedy in writing is the textbook (a)(14) violation. See *Meyer v. Sprint Spectrum L.P.* (2009) 45 Cal.4th 634, 639–40.

## § 1770(a)(5) — Misrepresenting Characteristics/Benefits
Prohibits representing services have "characteristics, … uses, benefits, or quantities that they do not have."

**Application:** 3/15/2026 "Message From Mary" CEO email and 3/21/2026 "Sunrun Value Report" — sent while Sunrun knew (Case #18181148) the system was at zero — touted benefits the system was not delivering. The "system reboot"/"power cycling" advice represents that customer self-help can restore service when Sunrun's monitoring already confirmed hardware/metering failure requiring truck-roll.

## § 1782 30-Day Notice & Cure
- Certified-mail notice ≥ 30 days before filing damages claim (§ 1782(a)).
- Injunctive/declaratory relief and **restitution** available **without** notice (§ 1782(d)) — file simultaneously to lock the cure window without giving Sunrun a free do-over.
- Cure must be **unconditional**. *Benson v. Southern Cal. Auto Sales* (2015) 239 Cal.App.4th 1198, 1207–10.

**Strategic letter demands:** (a) restoration of 2017 system; (b) full SDG&E true-up reimbursement; (c) PPA § 8 production refund; (d) confirmation of EPG for both contracts; (e) attorney-fee reservation.
