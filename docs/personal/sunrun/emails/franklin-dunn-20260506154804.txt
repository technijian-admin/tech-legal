Under the Monthly Service Agreement, Vintage Design was charged in advance for the estimated tech support services (“Tech Services”) provided by Technijian.  Tech Services are broken into three categories:  

1.      Offshore Support (OS Support aka “Offshort_Support.R” or “CHD-TS1:Support:N/Remote”); 

2.      Offshore Support – After Hours (OS Support.AH aka “Offshore_Support.R.A.F” or “CHD-TS1:Support:AH/Remote”); and 

3.      Onshore Support (Tech Support aka, “Tech_Support.R” or “IRV-TS1:Support:N/Remote”)

 

The ShareFile includes Monthly Invoices for the period from May 2023 through July 2025.  Please note the following for each Invoice.The invoices are dated a month in advance of the month in which the onshore and offshore Tech Services were to be provided. So, the invoice dated June 1, 2024, for example, is the invoice that includes the Tech Services for the month of July 2024.  This was done to ensure payment for Tech Services was provided on the first day of each month, in advance.  

The ShareFile also includes Excel Spreadsheets, Monthly Time Entries, that detail the hours of Tech Support services reported by Technician each month.    The Monthly Time entries are dated a month in arrears. So, for example, the actual hours worked for the month of July 2024 are included in the Monthly Time Entries dated in August 2024. To calculate the hours of Tech Supported reported each month, you will need to look to columns H, J and N.  Column N distinguishes between CHD-TS1 (offshore), IRV-TS1 (onshore), and other services that were provided to Vintage Design. For CHD-TS1, these services include both regular hours and after hours. So, to calculate the CHD-TS1 hours, look to column H (for normal hours) and column J (for After Hours). 

Weekly Invoices and Excel Spreadsheets of Weekly Time EntriesSimilar to the prior described documents. They were prepared and sent weekly. Please note, the weekly time records are the most accurate record of the hourly Tech Support services provided. They include time worked that does not make it onto the monthly time entry spreadsheets due to timing issues. Meaning, a monthly time entry spreadsheet is completed on a particular day at the end of a month. There are time entries that are reported after that monthly report is generated. As a result, there are generally more hours worked each month than are reported on the monthly time entry reports.     

The agreement with Vintage Design was an Under-Contract 12 Month Period program. Therefore, following each 12-month period, the actual hours known at the time were added together for each category of service (onshore regular, offshore regular, and offshore after hours), divided by 12, and this was the new amount that would be invoiced monthly for the following Under-Contract 12 Month Period.
