## Track 2: Small Claims Court — Primary Enforcement Track
California Superior Court, Orange County — Small Claims Division.

**Key facts:**
- Limit: $12,500 for individuals in CA (your claim ~$3,600 is well within)
- Filing fee: ~$75 (may be waived based on income)
- No lawyer allowed on either side at hearing (levels the playing field)
- Filing form: **SC-100** (online or at Lamoreaux Justice Center, Orange)
- Expected hearing: 30-75 days after filing

1. Wait 14 days from 2026-04-17 formal demand (expires ~2026-05-01)
2. Calculate exact damages from SDG&E bills:

3. Gather evidence folder: bills, emails, case #18181148, formal demand, Sunrun's $450 offer
