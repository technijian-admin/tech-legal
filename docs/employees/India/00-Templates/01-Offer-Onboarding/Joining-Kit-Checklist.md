## 1. Identity & Address Proof

- [ ] PAN Card (mandatory)
- [ ] Aadhaar Card (mandatory; full Aadhaar to be furnished only through UIDAI-compliant secure channel for EPF/ESIC)
- [ ] Passport (if available)
- [ ] Voter ID or Driving Licence (as supplementary ID)
- [ ] 4 recent passport-size colour photographs (white background)
- [ ] Current address proof (utility bill / rental agreement / bank statement — not older than 3 months)
- [ ] Permanent address proof

## 2. Educational Qualifications

- [ ] Class X (SSC) marksheet and certificate
- [ ] Class XII (HSC) marksheet and certificate
- [ ] Graduation — all semester marksheets + consolidated marksheet + degree certificate
- [ ] Post-graduation / Doctorate — marksheets + degree (if applicable)
- [ ] Professional certifications (e.g., MCSE, CCNA, AWS, Azure, PMP, Six Sigma)
- [ ] Diploma / vocational certificates (if applicable)

## 3. Previous Employment — for each prior employer

- [ ] Offer letter / Appointment letter
- [ ] Relieving letter
- [ ] Experience letter
- [ ] Last 3 months' salary slips (each employer)
- [ ] **Form 16** — last 2 financial years
- [ ] Resignation acceptance / No-Dues certificate
- [ ] Last-drawn salary bank statement (last 3 months)

## 4. Statutory & Banking

- [ ] **UAN (Universal Account Number)** — for EPF transfer under the EPF & MP Act, 1952
- [ ] Cancelled cheque OR bank passbook first page (salary account)
- [ ] **EPF Nomination — Form 2** (Revised) under EPF Scheme, 1952
- [ ] **Gratuity Nomination — Form F** under the Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972 and Rules, 1972
- [ ] **ESIC details / IP number** (if wages fall within statutory ESI threshold per ESI Act, 1948)
- [ ] Professional Tax declaration (as applicable under Haryana law)
- [ ] Investment declaration — Form 12BB (for TDS computation under Income Tax Act, 1961)
- [ ] Previous-employer income declaration — Form 12B (if joining mid-financial-year)

## 5. Medical

- [ ] Pre-employment medical fitness certificate (format will be provided)
- [ ] Group Mediclaim nomination form (self + dependants)
- [ ] Blood group declaration

## 6. Emergency Contact

- [ ] Emergency Contact Form — name, relationship, phone, email, address (2 contacts)
