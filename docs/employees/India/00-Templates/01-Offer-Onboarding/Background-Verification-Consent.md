## 3. Scope of Verification — Consent Items

I expressly authorise the Company and its authorised vendor(s) to conduct the following checks:

- [ ] **Identity verification** (PAN, Aadhaar, Passport, Voter ID, Driving Licence)
- [ ] **Current address verification**
- [ ] **Permanent address verification**
- [ ] **Education verification** — all qualifications from Class X onwards, including degree, institution, year, and mode of study
- [ ] **Professional certifications** verification (e.g., MCSE, CCNA, AWS, PMP, etc.)
- [ ] **Employment history verification** — past employers (minimum 3 or all within last 7 years, whichever is fewer), covering designation, tenure, compensation, reason for leaving, and eligibility for rehire
- [ ] **Reference checks** (professional)
- [ ] **Criminal record / court-record check** (national and state-level, civil and criminal)
- [ ] **Global database screening** (sanctions lists, PEP, watchlists, regulatory debarments)
- [ ] **Credit check** (for finance / fiduciary / cash-handling roles only)
- [ ] **Drug / substance screening** (role-dependent, as permitted by law)
- [ ] **Social-media footprint review** (public information only, non-intrusive)
- [ ] **Directorship / Companies Act search** (MCA records)

I understand and agree that verification may extend to any additional reasonable check required by the Company, its clients (under Master Services Agreements), or regulators, provided I am notified of any materially expanded scope.
