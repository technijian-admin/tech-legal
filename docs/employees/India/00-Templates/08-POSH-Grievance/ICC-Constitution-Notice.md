## 1. Legal Basis

Pursuant to **Section 4 of the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013** ("POSH Act") read with the POSH Rules, 2013, Technijian IT Services Pvt. Ltd. ("**Company**") hereby constitutes the Internal Committee ("**IC**" — also referred to as the Internal Complaints Committee / "ICC") for the purpose of receiving and redressing complaints of sexual harassment at the workplace.

Nomenclature note: The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Amendment effected a re-styling of the committee from "Internal Complaints Committee" to "Internal Committee". Both terms refer to the same statutory body and are used interchangeably in Company documents.

## 3. Term of Office

Per **Section 4(3) POSH Act**, the Presiding Officer and every Member shall hold office for a period not exceeding three (3) years from the Effective Date stated above. Members may be nominated for further terms subject to statutory limits.

Any Member or the Presiding Officer may be removed from the IC prior to expiry of term in the circumstances set out in **Section 4(5) POSH Act**, including:
(a) contravention of Section 16 (confidentiality);
(b) conviction of a criminal offence or pending inquiry into any such offence;
(c) conviction for any offence involving moral turpitude;
(d) abuse of position so as to render continuance in office prejudicial to public interest.

## 4. How to Reach the IC

- **Primary email (monitored by all IC members):** `icc-india@technijian.com`
- **Complaint drop-box location:** Notice board at the Company's registered office — Panchkula, Haryana.
- **Direct contact:** Any IC member may be contacted directly using the details in the table above.
- **Assistance in drafting a complaint:** Per **Section 9(2) POSH Act**, any IC member shall render reasonable assistance to a complainant unable to put the complaint in writing.

---

## 5. Public Posting Requirement (Section 19(b) POSH Act)

A copy of this Notice, showing the names and contact details of IC members and the penal consequences of sexual harassment, shall be conspicuously displayed at:

1. The Company notice board at the registered office, Panchkula, Haryana.
2. The Company intranet / HRMS landing page.
3. All office locations, including client sites where Company employees are deployed on long-term assignment.
4. Circulated by email to every employee on joining and on any change in composition.

---

## 6. Annual Report

Per **Section 21 POSH Act**, the IC shall prepare an annual report in the prescribed form and submit the same to the Company. The Company shall forward the report to the **District Officer, Panchkula, Haryana** by the end of January of the following calendar year, and shall include the prescribed particulars in the Company's annual report under **Section 22**.
