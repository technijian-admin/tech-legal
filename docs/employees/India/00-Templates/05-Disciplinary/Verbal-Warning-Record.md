## 7. Escalation Path If Issue Recurs

The Company follows a progressive disciplinary ladder to ensure natural justice is observed at every step, consistent with the Industrial Employment (Standing Orders) Act, 1946 (as applicable) and judicial guidance from the Supreme Court of India (*Workmen of Motipur Sugar Factory v. State of Bihar*; *Indian Iron & Steel Co. Ltd. v. Workmen*):

1. **Step 1 — Verbal Warning (Counseling)** — this record.
2. **Step 2 — Written Warning** (Ref: DISC/YYYY/NN).
3. **Step 3 — Show Cause Notice** (Ref: SCN/YYYY/NN).
4. **Step 4 — Suspension Pending Inquiry + Charge Sheet** (Ref: SUSP/YYYY/NN, CS/YYYY/NN).
5. **Step 5 — Notice of Domestic Inquiry** (Ref: INQ/YYYY/NN).
6. **Step 6 — Impartial Inquiry by Inquiry Officer.**
7. **Step 7 — Review of Inquiry Report by Disciplinary Authority.**
8. **Step 8 — Final Warning OR Termination for Cause** (Ref: FW/YYYY/NN or TERM/YYYY/NN).
9. **Step 9 — Right of Appeal to Appellate Authority** within 30 days.

## 8. Confidentiality

This record is confidential and is to be filed in the employee personnel file under the custody of HR. Access is limited to HR, the Disciplinary Authority, and the Appellate Authority.

## 9. Retention

This record shall be retained on the employee file for **12 months** from the date above. If no further disciplinary action is warranted within that period, the record may be expunged on the employee's written request.
