I, the undersigned, hereby acknowledge and confirm the following:

1. I have received, read, and understood the **Technijian IT Services Pvt. Ltd. Employee Handbook** and the **Code of Conduct** (collectively, the "Code").

2. I understand that the Code sets out the minimum standards of behavior expected of me, including but not limited to:
   - **Honesty, integrity, and compliance with law** — adherence to all applicable Indian laws, regulations, and Company policies;
   - **Equal treatment and non-discrimination** — in accordance with the Equal Remuneration Act, 1976 and Articles 14, 15, and 16 of the Constitution of India;
   - **Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH)** — in accordance with the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013 and the Rules thereunder; I commit to participating in POSH training and treating every colleague with respect;
   - **Anti-bribery and anti-corruption** — in accordance with the **Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988** (as amended by the Prevention of Corruption (Amendment) Act, 2018); I will not offer, give, solicit, or accept any bribe, kickback, or improper advantage in any form;
   - **Data protection and confidentiality** — in accordance with the Information Technology Act, 2000, the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, my Non-Disclosure Agreement, and the IT Acceptable Use Policy;
   - **Conflict of interest disclosure** — I will disclose, in writing to HR, any actual, potential, or perceived conflict of interest (including personal financial interests, relationships with clients/vendors, outside employment, and directorships);
   - **Anti-moonlighting** — I will not undertake any secondary employment, consultancy, or business activity without the prior written consent of the Company;
   - **Responsible use of Company property and IT systems** — including laptops, mobile devices, networks, software, data, and credentials;
   - **Fair dealing** — with clients, vendors, colleagues, regulators, and all stakeholders;
   - **Health, safety, and well-being** — compliance with all applicable safety rules at the workplace.

3. I understand that any violation of the Code may result in **disciplinary action up to and including termination for cause**, and that certain acts may also attract **civil or criminal liability** under Indian law.

4. I confirm that I will **promptly report**, in good faith, any actual or suspected violation, conflict of interest, or unethical conduct through:
   - My line manager;
   - HR at **hr-india@technijian.com**;
   - In case of sexual harassment: the Internal Committee at **icc-india@technijian.com**;
   - The Company's whistleblower channel (see the Employee Handbook for the current dedicated email).

5. I acknowledge that the Company has a **non-retaliation policy** and that no action will be taken against any employee who reports a concern in good faith.

6. I understand that this Acknowledgement will be placed in my personnel file, and that the Code may be amended from time to time at the Company's discretion, with revisions communicated through the Company's standard channels.
