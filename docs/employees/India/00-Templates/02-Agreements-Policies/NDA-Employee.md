**2.01** The Employee shall:

(a) use Confidential Information solely for the performance of duties assigned by the Company;
(b) not disclose Confidential Information to any third party, including family members, without the Company's prior written consent;

(e) promptly return or, at the Company's option, destroy all Confidential Information (including all copies, notes, and derivatives thereof) upon the earlier of termination of employment or the Company's written request, and certify such return or destruction in writing;
(f) continue to observe these confidentiality obligations after the termination of employment.

**2.02** The Employee's obligations under this Agreement are in addition to (and do not limit) obligations under the Indian Contract Act, 1872, the Information Technology Act, 2000, the Companies Act, 2013, and any other applicable law.
