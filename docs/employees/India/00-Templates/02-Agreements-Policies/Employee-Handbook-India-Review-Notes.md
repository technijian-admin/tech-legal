## 3. Specific India-Law Gaps Commonly Present

1. **Old Maternity Benefit numbers (12 weeks)** → update to **26 weeks** for first two confinements (MB Amendment Act, 2017)
2. **Old ESI wage ceiling (Rs. 15,000)** → update to **Rs. 21,000/month** (since 2017)
3. **Missing creche clause** (required if ≥50 employees under MB Amendment Act, 2017 Sec 11A)
4. **POSH ICC not named / external member missing** — statutory risk; fine up to Rs. 50,000
5. **No reference to DPDP Act, 2023** (Digital Personal Data Protection Act — notified in phases; employee-data processing needs updated consent framework)
6. **No reference to Labour Codes (Code on Wages 2019; Code on Social Security 2020; Code on Industrial Relations 2020; OSH Code 2020)** — even though not yet fully notified, handbook should flag "pending notification" and commit to updating
7. **Gratuity — no Form F nomination reference** — Nomination under Rule 6 of the Payment of Gratuity Rules, 1972 is mandatory at joining
8. **EPF — no UAN activation / passbook self-service reference**
9. **Haryana Labour Welfare Fund Act, 1981** — contribution rarely mentioned
10. **Equal Remuneration Act, 1976** — anti-discrimination section often missing statutory citation
11. **Industrial Employment (Standing Orders) Act, 1946** — disciplinary framework rarely anchors to this
12. **Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 + 2018 amendment** — anti-bribery often weak; IT-sector vendor kickbacks common; amendment introduced offense for commercial organizations
13. **Punjab Shops & Commercial Establishments Act, 1958** (applicable in Haryana / Panchkula) — working hours / leave should cite this by name
14. **At-will language bleed** — sometimes pasted from US parent handbook; must be scrubbed
15. **US jurisdiction / California references** — scrub
