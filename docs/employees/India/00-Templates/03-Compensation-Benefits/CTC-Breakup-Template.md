## 3. Statutory and Policy Notes

1. **Employer PF (EPF & MP Act, 1952):** Employer contributes 12% of Basic Salary. Statutory wage ceiling under the Act is INR 15,000/month; above this ceiling, employer contribution may be restricted to INR 1,800/month unless the Company voluntarily extends contribution on actual Basic, as permitted by the scheme. The chosen approach for this employee is: [ ] statutory ceiling / [ ] voluntary on actual Basic.

2. **ESI (ESI Act, 1948):** Applicable only where gross wages are ≤ INR 21,000/month. Employer 3.25% and Employee 0.75%. On ceasing to be ESI-eligible, the employee is migrated to Group Mediclaim (Component J).

3. **Gratuity (Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972):** Provisioned at approximately 4.81% of Basic Salary as part of CTC. Actual payment arises only on completion of five (5) years of continuous service, or earlier on death or disablement as provided in the Act. Gratuity is not payable as part of monthly salary.

4. **Statutory Bonus (Payment of Bonus Act, 1965):** Payable to employees whose Basic wages are ≤ INR 21,000/month, at a minimum of 8.33% and maximum of 20% of Basic wages, subject to the Act. Above the threshold, any bonus is discretionary/performance-linked.

5. **Professional Tax — Haryana:** As on date, Haryana has **not** notified a State Professional Tax; the field is retained for future applicability. Employees working in other States (e.g., Maharashtra, Karnataka, West Bengal) will be subject to the P-Tax of the relevant State.

6. **Haryana Labour Welfare Fund Act, 1965:** Nominal employee and employer contribution as notified by the Government of Haryana from time to time.

7. **Code on Wages, 2019 and Code on Social Security, 2020:** Upon notification and enforcement of these Codes, the salary structure, statutory deductions and bonus computation will be re-aligned to comply. Company reserves the right to restructure CTC components to remain compliant without reducing total CTC.

8. **Payment of Wages Act, 1936:** Salary is paid by the end of each wage period, by electronic bank transfer into the employee's designated account.

9. **Restructuring:** The Company may, with reasonable notice, restructure the break-up of components (Basic, HRA, allowances, etc.) for tax or regulatory reasons, without reducing total CTC.

10. **Reimbursements:** Any amounts shown under mobile/internet/fuel/books are reimbursements against bills, governed by the Reimbursement Policy, and are **not** part of fixed CTC.
