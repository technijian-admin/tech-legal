## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

### 1.1 Project Title

My SEO Program — Validation SEO for Institutional Multifamily Capital

### 1.2 Project Description

VisionWise Capital, LLC ("VWC") is a Southern California commercial real estate investment firm operating a syndicated multifamily fund with a hard-cap leverage policy (sub-50% Loan-to-Value), serving accredited investors, Registered Investment Advisors (RIAs), and high-net-worth individuals. Because VWC's principal is an SEC-registered investment adviser, every published word falls under SEC Marketing Rule 206(4)-1 oversight, and VWC's growth depends on a digital footprint that confirms institutional quality when sophisticated allocators conduct due diligence.

Technijian will deliver its **My SEO** program — a 12-month managed SEO retainer with unlimited monthly service hours — engineered for VWC's "Validation SEO" posture rather than retail lead generation. The engagement is **structured in two pricing phases**: Months 1 through 3 deliver the website, technical SEO, and content foundation at $1,000 per month; Months 4 through 12 layer on AI search optimization, content syndication, and PR releases at $1,550 per month, by which time the foundation is complete and amplification can produce results without burning spend on an empty backbone.

The first day of work is May 1, 2026 and the initial term runs through April 30, 2027.

## 2. SCOPE OF WORK

### 2.1 In Scope — My SEO Program

Technijian shall provide the following recurring services for the 12-month term, with the timing of each component as specified in Section 3:

**Foundation (Months 1 through 12) — My SEO Tier 3:**

- **Website Design and Hosting** — Custom-designed, responsive, SEO-optimized WordPress website. Hosting for two (2) WordPress sites (one production, one development), 100 GB of storage, daily backups, ongoing security updates, and core/plugin maintenance.
- **SEO Optimization** — Keyword research, on-page SEO (content, meta tags, internal linking, image optimization), off-page SEO (high-quality backlink building), monthly analytics reporting, competitive SERP analysis, and SEO strategy refinement.
- **Content Production — Blogs for Five (5) Keywords** — Five (5) keyword-targeted blog posts per month written for VWC's institutional audience, with a content calendar, internal-linking strategy, and post-publication amplification across VWC's own channels.

**Authority and Amplification Add-Ons (Months 4 through 12 only):**

- **AI Search Optimization** — Schema markup, semantic SEO clusters, featured-snippet targeting, entity optimization for VWC and Sanford Coggins, technical SEO improvements for AI/LLM readability, brand-mention building, AI-driven keyword and topic research, competitor gap analysis, content refreshes for AI indexing, and reporting on AI-search performance.
- **PR Releases (Published Quarterly)** — Pre-approved 500+ word releases distributed to 300+ live placements per quarter, including FOX, ABC, and NBC affiliates and digitaljournal.com (DR 87 / DA 88), with three (3) body links and one (1) bio link per release, comprehensive submission report, and natural anchor optimization.
- **Content Syndication** — Monthly distribution of approved long-form content to 250+ websites, coverage in media outlets and news channels, premium syndication outlets, second-tier link building with canonical/original-credit links, and detailed monthly distribution reporting.

**Reporting and Account Management (Months 1 through 12):**

- Monthly KPI report with baseline-to-current metrics for organic traffic, branded SERP coverage, keyword rankings, Core Web Vitals, backlinks acquired, and content shipped.
- Quarterly business review with VWC leadership.
- Dedicated Technijian SEO pod with unlimited monthly service hours during the term.

- **Outside compliance counsel review fees** — Technijian will route content requiring securities-law review through Client's designated counsel; counsel time is billed by counsel directly to Client.

- **24x7 system monitoring, production incident response, or managed IT services** (available under a separate Technijian MSA).

Items above outside My SEO are typically delivered via separate per-workstream SOWs or a monthly consulting retainer; pricing for those is discussed only after this SOW is in effect.

## 3. SERVICE PHASES AND DELIVERY SCHEDULE

The 12-month term is organized into two consecutive phases. There is no gap between phases; the transition from Phase 1 to Phase 2 happens automatically at the start of Month 4 (August 1, 2026) without further authorization.

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1 through 3 — May 1, 2026 through July 31, 2026)

**3.1.1 Description**

Months 1 through 3 build the digital foundation that every later authority and amplification activity depends on. Add-ons (AI Search Optimization, PR Releases, Content Syndication) intentionally do not run during this phase because there is no foundation yet for them to amplify.

**3.1.2 Monthly Recurring Activities**

- Website design, build, and hosting setup (Month 1 launch focus).
- Technical SEO audit, Core Web Vitals remediation, schema baseline.
- Index cleansing (de-index or rewrite content that conflicts with Validation SEO posture).
- Keyword research and competitive SERP baseline.
- On-page SEO across all production pages.
- Backlink building (high-quality, institutional-tier referring domains).
- Five (5) keyword-targeted blog posts per month with content-calendar planning.
- Monthly KPI report.

**3.1.3 Phase 1 Deliverables**

- Production WordPress site live on Technijian-hosted infrastructure by end of Month 1, with development site mirroring production.
- Baseline metrics report by end of Week 2 of Month 1 (organic traffic, indexed pages, branded SERP coverage, Core Web Vitals, current backlink profile, current keyword rankings).
- Existing-asset inventory and classification (keep-amplify / rewrite / de-index / suppress) by end of Month 1.
- Knowledge Panel claim and optimization for VWC firm and Sanford Coggins by end of Month 2.
- Fifteen (15) blog posts published over Phase 1 (5 per month × 3 months).
- Monthly KPI reports at end of Months 1, 2, and 3.

### Phase 2: Foundation Plus Authority and Amplification (Months 4 through 12 — August 1, 2026 through April 30, 2027)

**3.2.1 Description**

Once the foundation is live, Phase 2 layers on the AI Search Optimization, quarterly PR Releases, and monthly Content Syndication that drive authority signals, branded SERP coverage, and institutional-tier referring domains. Phase 1 activities continue throughout Phase 2.

**3.2.2 Monthly Recurring Activities (Phase 1 Activities Continue, Plus)**

- AI Search Optimization — schema markup, semantic SEO clusters, featured-snippet targeting, entity optimization for VWC and Sanford Coggins, AI/LLM citation tracking and reporting.
- Content Syndication — monthly distribution of approved long-form content to 250+ websites, coverage in media outlets and news channels, second-tier link building with canonical/original-credit links, monthly distribution report.
- Quarterly PR Releases — pre-approved 500+ word releases at the start of Months 4, 7, and 10, distributed to 300+ live placements (FOX/ABC/NBC affiliates, digitaljournal.com), with submission report.

**3.2.3 Phase 2 Deliverables**

- Three (3) PR Releases over Phase 2 (Q1 of Phase 2 / Month 4, Q2 / Month 7, Q3 / Month 10).
- Nine (9) months of Content Syndication distribution reports (one per month, Months 4 through 12).
- Forty-five (45) additional blog posts published (5 per month × 9 months).
- AI Search Optimization performance report monthly, included in the monthly KPI report.
- End-of-term performance summary at end of Month 12 with recommendations for the renewal term.

(e) **Lien on Work Product.** Technijian shall retain a lien on all deliverables, work product, documentation, and materials (excluding Client Data and any administrative credentials, root accounts, DNS, or break-glass information) under this SOW until all amounts owed are paid in full.
