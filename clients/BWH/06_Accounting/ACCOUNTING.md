1. **The 687.67 figure on the 4/1 email is almost certainly wrong.** The email header carried `MonthStartDate=3/1/2026`, which suggests it was pulled as a single-period snapshot rather than life-of-contract cumulative unpaid. Our portal data does not contain any rolling window or month-specific extract that produces 687.67 — it is not the cumulative through any valid cutoff.

2. **Ravi's reply effectively says the 4/1 email was wrong.** The 1,011.69 figure Ravi is now quoting as the "before April 1st total hours" is different from what Tharunaa sent Dave on 4/1. Dave has only seen the 687.67 number and the 1,040.28 number, and the gap between them is 352.61 — which cannot be explained by 23 days of delivery (~100 hrs delivered actual).
