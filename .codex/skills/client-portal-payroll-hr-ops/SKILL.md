# Client Portal Payroll & HR Ops

Use this skill for payroll and employee-operation workflows.

## Workflow

1. Search with `payroll`, `bank info`, `india emp`, `eom approval`, or `payroll slip`.
2. Read [references/coverage.md](references/coverage.md).
