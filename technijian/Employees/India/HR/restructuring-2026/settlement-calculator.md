## Tax treatment summary

- **Retrenchment compensation**: Taxable as profit in lieu of salary; partial exemption under §10(10B) Income-tax Act
- **EL encashment**: Taxable as salary; lifetime exemption up to ₹25 lakh under §10(10AA) for non-government employees
- **Notice pay-in-lieu**: Taxable as salary
- **Re-skilling Fund**: Not paid to employee; employer expense
- **TDS**: Technijian must deduct at applicable slab on all of the above before paying
