# Haryana — Labour & Employment Law (applicable to Panchkula)

**Last verified:** 2026-05-06.

This folder covers the **state-level labour rules applicable to a Technijian establishment located in Panchkula, Haryana**.

Panchkula is a district city in Haryana state. Although Panchkula is geographically adjacent to Chandigarh UT, **it is in a different jurisdiction** — Haryana state laws apply, not Chandigarh UT or Punjab.

Haryana adopted the Punjab Shops and Commercial Establishments Act, 1958 at the time of state formation in 1966 and has since amended it. Haryana also has its own state acts on welfare fund, festival holidays, women's employment in night shifts, and (controversially) the now-struck-down Local Candidates Act.

## Key statutes / notifications applicable in Haryana / Panchkula

### State acts

| Act | What it governs |
|---|---|
| **Punjab Shops and Commercial Establishments Act, 1958** (adopted by Haryana) | Primary state act for IT/ITeS offices |
| **Punjab Shops and Commercial Establishments Rules, 1958** (Haryana-amended; especially Rule 15) | Operational rules including women night shifts |
| **Haryana Industrial Establishments (National and Festival Holidays) Act, 1971** | Mandatory paid holidays |
| **Haryana Labour Welfare Fund Act, 1973** (operationalises Punjab LWF Act 1965 in Haryana) | LWF contribution |
| **Haryana State Employment of Local Candidates Act, 2020** | **STRUCK DOWN** — see [local-candidates-act.md](local-candidates-act.md) |
| **Haryana State Industrial and Employment Policy 2020** (IT/ITeS sector) | Policy-level concessions |
| **Haryana Right to Service Act, 2014** | Service-delivery deadlines for licences |

### Recent notifications (most relevant)

- **8-May-2025**: Revised conditions for engaging women employees during night shifts (8:00 PM to 6:00 AM) — see [women-night-shift.md](women-night-shift.md).
- **9-April-2026**: Notification No. 2/25/26-2 Lab fixing revised minimum wages effective 1-April-2026 — see [minimum-wages.md](minimum-wages.md).
- **April 2026**: Relaxation of fixed working hours under Shops and Establishments Act (commercial establishments).

### Central acts (apply directly)

- The four labour codes (2019/2020) — see [01-labour-codes-overview.md](../01-labour-codes-overview.md).
- POSH Act 2013, Apprentices Act 1961, Child Labour Act 1986 (as amended), DPDP Act 2023.
