# Chandigarh UT — Employer Registrations and Licences

**Last verified:** 2026-05-06.

This page lists every registration / licence a Technijian establishment in Chandigarh UT must obtain, the issuing authority, and the operational handle. Use as a setup-to-steady-state inventory.

---

## Registration matrix

| # | Registration / Licence | Authority | Threshold / when required | Validity | Penalty for non-registration |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Shops & Establishments Registration** under Punjab S&E Act 1958 (extended) | Inspector of Shops, Chandigarh Administration | Within **30 days** of starting business (any number of employees) | 3 years | ₹500–₹2,000 + ₹100/day continued |
| 2 | **Permanent Account Number (PAN)** of the establishment / company | Income Tax Department | At incorporation | Permanent | Penalty under IT Act |
| 3 | **Tax Deduction & Collection Account Number (TAN)** | Income Tax Department | When TDS applies (always for employer) | Permanent | ₹10,000 |
| 4 | **GST Registration** (if turnover > ₹20 lakh / interstate / IT services as inter-state supply) | CBIC (Central) + Chandigarh State | When threshold crossed / from inception for IT/ITeS exporters | Continuous | Higher of ₹10,000 or 10% of tax |
| 5 | **EPF Registration** | EPFO Regional Office, Chandigarh | When **≥20 employees** (sticky after) | Continuous | Damages 5–25% of unpaid + interest 12% |
| 6 | **ESI Registration** | ESIC Sub-Regional Office, Chandigarh | When **≥10 employees** | Continuous | Damages + interest |
| 7 | **Professional Tax Registration** | Chandigarh has **NO** professional tax (unlike Haryana) — NOT applicable in Chandigarh UT | — | — | — |
| 8 | **Punjab Labour Welfare Fund** registration | Welfare Commissioner, Chandigarh | When **≥5 employees** | Continuous | Penalty + interest under §16 |
| 9 | **OSH Code Registration** | Online portal (state) | When **≥10 workers** | Continuous (one-time) | Up to ₹50,000 |
| 10 | **Apprentices Portal Registration** (NATS / NAPS) | Directorate General of Training | When **≥30 employees** | Continuous | ₹500/day per default |
| 11 | **POSH Internal Committee** (employer's order; not a "registration" but a compliance action) | Employer's own constitution + filing of annual report with District Officer | When **≥10 employees** | IC term: 3 years | ₹50,000 + repeat doubles |
| 12 | **Chandigarh Pollution Control Committee** consent (if applicable) | CPCC Chandigarh | If activities require environmental consent — IT/ITeS offices typically exempt | — | Air/Water Acts penalties |
| 13 | **Trade Licence** (if shop / commercial unit visible to public) | Municipal Corporation Chandigarh | Sometimes required by municipality | Annual | Municipal fine |
| 14 | **Fire NOC** | Chandigarh Fire & Emergency Services | Premises with > 500 sq m or as per building plan | Annual / 3-year | Closure / fine |

## Operational details by registration

### 1. Shops & Establishments — Form A

**Filing**:
- Form A (statement) + supporting docs:
  - Identity proof of employer / authorised signatory.
  - PAN of establishment.
  - Lease deed / ownership of premises.
  - Photo of establishment.
  - Manager's appointment letter (if any).
  - List of employees with designations.

**Fee**: ~₹100 to ₹2,000+ depending on employee count (verify current schedule).

**Where**: Office of Inspector of Shops, Chandigarh Administration. Online portal (where active): [chandigarh.gov.in](https://chandigarh.gov.in/) > Department of Labour > Online services.

**Renewal**: Form B before 31-March of expiry year. ₹100–₹500 late fee per year if missed.

### 5. EPF — UAN / Employer code

**Pre-condition**: Establishment has ≥20 employees, OR voluntary coverage.

**Steps**:
1. Establishment registers on EPFO unified portal: [unifiedportal-emp.epfindia.gov.in](https://unifiedportal-emp.epfindia.gov.in/).
2. Employer Establishment Code allotted.
3. Each employee gets UAN (Universal Account Number).
4. Monthly ECR (Electronic Challan-cum-Return) filed by 15th of next month.
5. Annual return: Form 6A (consolidated).

**Compliance vendor**: many companies use Razorpay Payroll, GreyTHR, Keka, Quick HR for end-to-end ECR filing.

### 6. ESI — Form 1

**Pre-condition**: ≥10 employees in a notified area (Chandigarh UT is fully notified).

**Steps**:
1. Registration on ESIC unified portal: [esic.in](https://www.esic.in/).
2. Employer Code (17-digit) allotted.
3. Each eligible employee (gross wages ≤₹21,000) gets Insurance Number (IP number).
4. Monthly contribution challan by 15th of next month.
5. Half-yearly returns: Apr-Sep and Oct-Mar.

### 8. Punjab Labour Welfare Fund (Chandigarh)

**Pre-condition**: ≥5 employees.

**Steps**:
1. Registration application to Welfare Commissioner.
2. Establishment code allotted.
3. Half-yearly remittance via challan + Form A return (deadlines: 15-Oct and 15-Apr).
4. Employee-wise contribution register maintained.

### 9. OSH Code Online Registration

**Pre-condition**: ≥10 workers (commercial establishment).

**Steps**:
1. Online portal of Ministry of Labour & Employment / state portal (when launched).
2. Single registration replaces previous multiple registrations under repealed acts.
3. Currently transitional: existing S&E registration treated as proxy until OSH portal fully live.

### 10. Apprentices

**Pre-condition**: ≥30 employees.

**Steps**:
1. Register on [apprenticeshipindia.gov.in](https://apprenticeshipindia.gov.in/).
2. Identify trades / qualifications in which apprentices will be engaged.
3. Define number of apprenticeship slots (2.5%–15% of strength).
4. Engage apprentices via the portal.
5. File quarterly progress reports.
6. Pay stipend per Government scale.

## Authorities and contacts

### Chandigarh Administration — Department of Labour

**Address (typical)**: Office of the Labour Commissioner-cum-Chief Inspector of Shops, Sector 17 / Sector 19, Chandigarh.
**Website**: [chandigarh.gov.in](https://chandigarh.gov.in/) → Departments → Labour.

**Officers** (verify current incumbents):
- Labour Commissioner-cum-Chief Inspector of Shops.
- Assistant Labour Commissioner.
- Inspectors of Shops (territorial allocation).

### EPFO — Regional Office, Chandigarh

**Address**: Bhavishya Nidhi Bhawan, Sector 17, Chandigarh (typical address; verify current).
**Website**: [epfindia.gov.in](https://www.epfindia.gov.in/).
**Phone**: Helpline 14470.

### ESIC — Sub-Regional Office, Chandigarh

**Address**: Panchdeep Bhawan, Sector 19, Chandigarh (typical; verify).
**Website**: [esic.gov.in](https://www.esic.gov.in/).

### Industrial Tribunal–cum–Labour Court, Chandigarh

For adjudication of industrial disputes under the IR Code 2020.
**Address (typical)**: Industrial Tribunal, Sector 9, Chandigarh.

### District Officer for POSH

Typically the Deputy Commissioner of Chandigarh receives the IC annual report under §21 of the POSH Act.

## Display requirements at the establishment

The following must be **prominently displayed** at every Technijian Chandigarh office:

| # | Display item | Source |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Shops & Establishments Registration Certificate | §14, Punjab S&E Act |
| 2 | Notice of weekly closure day | §11, Punjab S&E Act |
| 3 | List of public holidays for the calendar year | Holidays Act 1965 |
| 4 | Statutory abstract of Punjab S&E Act | Rules |
| 5 | Statutory abstract of Code on Wages 2019 | Code on Wages Rules |
| 6 | Statutory abstract of Maternity Benefit chapter (Social Security Code) | Rules |
| 7 | Statutory abstract of POSH Act 2013 + IC composition + contact | §19, POSH Act |
| 8 | Notice of working hours (start, close, rest interval) | §15, Punjab S&E Act |
| 9 | Minimum wages notification of Chandigarh UT | Code on Wages |
| 10 | Equal Remuneration / non-discrimination notice | Chapter V, Code on Wages |
| 11 | EPF / ESI subscriber notice | Social Security Code |
| 12 | Emergency exit / fire-safety plan | OSH Code + Fire NOC |
| 13 | First-aid box location | OSH Code |
| 14 | Anti-child labour notice | Child Labour Act |
