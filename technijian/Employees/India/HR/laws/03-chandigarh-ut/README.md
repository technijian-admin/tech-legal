# Chandigarh Union Territory — Labour & Employment Law

**Last verified:** 2026-05-06.

This folder covers the **state-level labour rules applicable to a Technijian establishment located in Chandigarh UT**.

Chandigarh is a **Union Territory** administered directly by the Government of India through an Administrator. It does not have a state legislature; instead, it has historically **adopted Punjab state acts** (because Chandigarh was carved out of Punjab in 1966 as a UT to serve as the joint capital of Punjab and Haryana). Subsequent labour-law notifications are issued by the **Chandigarh Administration**.

## Key statutes / notifications applicable in Chandigarh UT

### State-extended acts

| Act | Source | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Punjab Shops and Commercial Establishments Act, 1958** | Punjab Act 15 of 1958, extended to Chandigarh by notification | Primary act governing IT/ITeS offices |
| **Punjab Industrial Establishments (National and Festival Holidays) Act, 1965** | Extended to Chandigarh | Mandatory paid holidays |
| **Punjab Labour Welfare Fund Act, 1965** | Extended to Chandigarh | Welfare-fund contribution |
| **Industrial Disputes (Chandigarh) Rules, 1968** | Procedural rules | Now to be read with IR Code 2020 |

### Central acts (apply directly)

- The four labour codes (2019/2020) — see [01-labour-codes-overview.md](../01-labour-codes-overview.md).
- POSH Act 2013, Apprentices Act 1961, Child Labour Act 1986 (as amended 2016), DPDP Act 2023.

### Chandigarh-Administration notifications

- Chandigarh UT minimum wages (under Code on Wages §8) — revised approximately twice yearly, typically aligned to Punjab.
- Notifications relating to women's night shifts, public holidays, working hours, registration fees.

Source for current notifications: [Chandigarh Administration — Department of Labour](https://chandigarh.gov.in/) (Labour & Employment).

## Where to engage with the Government

| Authority | Function |
|---|---|
| **Labour Commissioner, Chandigarh Administration** | Registration of shops & establishments; minimum wages; LWF; inspections; conciliation; women's night-shift exemption |
| **Chief Inspector of Shops, Chandigarh** | Day-to-day inspection of S&E registered establishments |
| **EPFO Regional Office, Chandigarh** | EPF / EPS / EDLI compliance |
| **ESIC Sub-Regional Office, Chandigarh** | ESI compliance |
| **Industrial Tribunal–cum–Labour Court, Chandigarh** | Adjudication of industrial disputes (now under IR Code 2020) |
| **District Officer, Chandigarh** | Receives POSH annual report; constitutes Local Committee |
