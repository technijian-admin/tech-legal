# Central Labour & Employment Laws — Detailed Pages

These pages cover the **central (national) laws** that apply to Technijian's operations in **both** Chandigarh UT and Panchkula. State-specific overlays are in the [03-chandigarh-ut/](../03-chandigarh-ut/) and [04-haryana-panchkula/](../04-haryana-panchkula/) folders.

For implementation status of the four 2019/2020 codes, see [01-labour-codes-overview.md](../01-labour-codes-overview.md).
