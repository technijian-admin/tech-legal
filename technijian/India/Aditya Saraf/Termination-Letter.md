This letter is issued with reference to your Offer / Appointment Letter dated 10 January 2026 and the Employee Handbook (Clause: Probation & Termination) that governs your employment with Technijian IT Services Pvt. Ltd. (the "Company").

## 1. Employment Status

1.01 You joined the Company on **10 January 2026** as **Lead — Client Support Engineering** (a managerial / supervisory role) on an initial probationary period of three (3) months in accordance with your Appointment Letter and the Employee Handbook.

1.02 As no written Confirmation Letter was issued to you at the end of the initial probationary period, your probation stood extended in accordance with the Employee Handbook, and you have continued to be a probationary employee as on the date of this letter.

1.03 Given your role as a Lead with managerial / supervisory functions, your employment is governed primarily by the contractual terms of your Appointment Letter; the Industrial Relations Code, 2020 provisions applicable to "workmen" do not apply to your separation.

## 2. Business Rationale and Decision

2.01 Following the operational impact of the September 2024 cyber-security incident and the subsequent loss of four major client accounts in Q4 2024 and Q1 2025, the Company has undertaken a structural rightsizing of its India delivery function. This is a *bona fide* cost-reduction measure consistent with the principles set out by the Hon'ble Supreme Court in *Workmen of Sudder Office, Cinnamara v. Management* (1971), *Hindustan Lever Ltd. v. Their Workmen* (1973) and *Western India Match Co. Ltd. v. Workmen* (1973).

2.02 As a result of this rightsizing, the Lead — Client Support Engineering role is being eliminated and its responsibilities consolidated within the existing senior leadership of the CSE function.

## 3. Termination Decision

3.01 After careful consideration, the Company has decided to terminate your services, effective close of business on **31 May 2026** ("Termination Date"), in accordance with the termination clause of your Appointment Letter.

3.02 This decision is final.

3.03 Your Company email access, VPN, all administrator and elevated-privilege credentials, and physical premises access will stand revoked with effect from **09 May 2026** in keeping with the Company's standard practice for managerial separations.

## 4. Notice and Final Compensation

4.01 You are placed on paid leave-of-absence ("paid LOA") from **09 May 2026** through the Termination Date. You will continue to receive your regular salary for the month of May 2026 in full.

4.02 In accordance with your Appointment Letter, the Company is providing you with twenty-three (23) days served as paid notice from 09 May 2026 to 31 May 2026, plus an additional **five (5) days' salary in lieu of notice** to ensure full satisfaction of the contractual notice requirement.

4.03 As per the Employee Handbook, paid leave during probation does not accrue as Earned Leave. No EL encashment is payable.
