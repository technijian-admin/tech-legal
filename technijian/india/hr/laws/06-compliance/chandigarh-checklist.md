# Chandigarh UT — Compliance Checklist

**Last verified:** 2026-05-06.

Actionable compliance checklist for Technijian's Chandigarh UT establishment. Use as a quarterly self-audit. Mark each item Yes/No/NA, attach evidence.

---

## A. One-time / setup compliance

| # | Item | Authority | Evidence to retain |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Shops & Establishments Registration (Form A) | Inspector of Shops, Chandigarh Administration | Registration certificate (3-yr validity) |
| 2 | PAN of establishment | Income-tax Department | PAN card |
| 3 | TAN registration | Income-tax Department | TAN allotment letter |
| 4 | EPF registration (≥20 employees) | EPFO Regional Office, Chandigarh | Establishment Code letter |
| 5 | ESIC registration (≥10 employees) | ESIC Sub-Regional Office, Chandigarh | Employer Code letter |
| 6 | Punjab Labour Welfare Fund registration (≥5 employees) | Welfare Commissioner, Chandigarh | Registration code |
| 7 | OSH Code online registration (≥10 workers) | State portal | Acknowledgement |
| 8 | Apprentices Portal registration (≥30 employees) | DGT (Govt of India) | NATS/NAPS code |
| 9 | POSH Internal Committee constituted | Self / Employer | Written order; IC composition |
| 10 | Trade Licence (if Municipal Corporation requires) | MC Chandigarh | Trade licence |
| 11 | Fire NOC | Chandigarh Fire & Emergency Services | Fire NOC certificate |
| 12 | GST registration (if applicable) | CBIC + Chandigarh State | GST certificate |
| 13 | Income-tax — TDS deductor profile | Income-tax | TRACES login |

## H. As-and-when compliance

| # | Trigger | Action | Deadline |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | New employee joining | EPF Form 11; ESIC Form 1; gratuity nomination; IC orientation; HR Privacy Notice; offer letter execution | Day 1 |
| 2 | Employee exit | Final settlement; Form 16; gratuity (if eligible); EL encashment; relieving letter; experience certificate; UAN exit; ESIC closure | Within 2 working days (final settlement); 30 days (gratuity) |
| 3 | Change of registered address / ownership / headcount | Form-C update to S&E Inspector | Within **15 days** |
| 4 | Workplace accident / serious injury | Notify Inspector of Shops + EPFO/ESIC | Within 24-48 hrs |
| 5 | POSH complaint received | IC inquiry initiated | Within 7 days |
| 6 | Personal-data breach | Investigate + notify Data Protection Board + affected employees | Per DPDP rules (~72 hrs) |
| 7 | Change of CTC / wage structure | Reissue offer letter / appointment letter; update payroll | Effective date |
| 8 | Employee grievance | GRC review | Within 30 days |
| 9 | Inspector visit | Cooperate; produce records; obtain inspection note | At visit |
| 10 | Re-verify Chandigarh UT minimum wages notification | At each notification (Apr / Oct typical) | At notification |
