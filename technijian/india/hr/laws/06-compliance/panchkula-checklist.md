## I. Haryana-specific watchouts

1. **LWF rates higher than Chandigarh**: ₹93/employee/month vs ₹25 in Chandigarh — ensure payroll vendor reflects correctly.
2. **8 mandatory public holidays in Haryana** vs 7 in Chandigarh — ensure annual holiday list reflects correctly.
3. **Women's night-shift exemption** is **time-limited (1 year)** and must be **renewed** in advance — set calendar reminder at T+11 months.
4. **Local Candidates Act (HSELC)** — currently struck down; no compliance required, but **monitor SC appeal status** — re-verify quarterly.
5. **Operating hours**: Haryana issued April-2026 relaxation of fixed working hours — verify current notification at hrylabour.gov.in.
6. **Right to Service Act**: Haryana licensing services are time-bound. Use this to escalate if S&E or other registration is delayed.
7. **HSIIDC** if office is in IT Park: separate compliance with HSIIDC.
