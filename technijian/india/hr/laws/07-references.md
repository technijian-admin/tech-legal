## How to keep this knowledge base current

1. **Quarterly review**: re-verify minimum wages, public-holiday list, key notifications. Update "Last verified" date on each page.
2. **Subscribe to alerts**: at least 2–3 from the recommended list above.
3. **Maintain CHANGELOG**: when content is updated, log change at the top of the affected page.
4. **External counsel quarterly call**: schedule a 30-min review call with Indian employment counsel quarterly to flag developments.
5. **Annual external compliance audit**: engage a vendor (TeamLease RegTech, AKMG, etc.) annually for an independent audit.
