# Haryana / Panchkula — Employer Registrations and Licences

**Last verified:** 2026-05-06.

This page lists every registration / licence a Technijian establishment in Panchkula, Haryana must obtain. Use as a setup-to-steady-state inventory.

## Operational details by registration

### 1. Shops & Establishments — Panchkula

**Online filing**: [hrylabour.gov.in](https://hrylabour.gov.in/) → Online Services → Establishment Registration.

**Documents**:
- PAN of establishment
- Identity / address proof of employer / authorised signatory
- Lease / ownership of premises in Panchkula
- Photo of establishment
- Manager's appointment letter
- List of employees with designations
- GST registration (if applicable)

**Fee** (sliding scale, verify current):
- 1–10 employees: ₹100
- 11–25: ₹500
- 26–50: ₹1,000
- 51–100: ₹2,000
- 101–500: ₹5,000
- 501+: ₹10,000

**Renewal**: Form B online, before 31-March of expiry year.

**Service-delivery time**: Under Haryana Right to Service Act, 2014, S&E Registration must be issued within **a notified service period** (typically 14–30 days). Auto-rejection / appeal mechanism applies if delayed.

### 5. EPF — Panchkula

**Pre-condition**: ≥20 employees, OR voluntary.

**Sub-office**: While the EPFO Regional Office for North Haryana is in Faridabad, **Panchkula has a sub-office** that handles employer queries and member services.

**Steps**: Same as Chandigarh — register on [unifiedportal-emp.epfindia.gov.in](https://unifiedportal-emp.epfindia.gov.in/), allotted Establishment Code, monthly ECR by 15th of next month.

### 6. ESI — Panchkula

**Pre-condition**: ≥10 employees in notified area. Panchkula is fully ESIC-implemented.

**Branch Office**: Panchkula Branch Office under Faridabad Regional Office.

**Steps**: Register on [esic.in](https://www.esic.in/), Employer Code allotted, IP Number for each ≤₹21K-wage employee, monthly contribution by 15th of next month, half-yearly returns.

### 8. Haryana Labour Welfare Fund

**Pre-condition**: ≥10 employees.

**Steps**:
1. Online registration via [hrylabour.gov.in](https://hrylabour.gov.in/) (Welfare Board section).
2. Establishment code allotted.
3. Half-yearly remittance:
   - Apr–Sep period: by 15-October.
   - Oct–Mar period: by 15-April.
4. Form A return submitted.
5. Maintain employee-wise contribution register.

### 12. Women's Night-Shift Exemption

**Pre-condition**: Women employed during 8 PM – 6 AM (any number).

**Steps**:
1. Establish POSH IC; train members and employees.
2. Set up safety, transport, lighting, security, medical facility, group of 4 per shift.
3. Obtain written consent from each woman who will work nights.
4. Apply to **Labour Commissioner / Chief Inspector of Shops** **at least 30 days** before the date of intended exemption.
5. Receive exemption certificate (valid 1 year).
6. Apply for renewal at 11 months.

See full SOP at [women-night-shift.md](women-night-shift.md).

### 16. HSIIDC

If Technijian's Panchkula office is in an HSIIDC industrial estate (IT Park areas of Sector 3, IT Park Panchkula), additional compliance:
- Plot lease / rental agreement.
- Building plan approval.
- Compliance with HSIIDC infrastructure norms.
- Periodic returns to HSIIDC.

Verify whether the office is in HSIIDC territory — if so, also engage with HSIIDC liaison.

### 17. STPI Registration (optional, for 100% software exporters)

If Technijian Panchkula office is registered under **Software Technology Parks of India (STPI)** scheme as a 100% Export-Oriented Unit (EOU):
- Customs duty exemption on imports.
- Income-tax / GST benefits.
- Foreign-exchange repatriation simplification.
- STPI operational compliance: annual filings, export monitoring, etc.

If unregistered, no obligation. STPI is a benefits-driven choice.

## Authorities and contacts

### Haryana Labour Department

**Head Office**: 30 Bays Building, Sector 17, Chandigarh (Yes — Haryana state secretariat is in Chandigarh, despite Chandigarh being a UT).
**Online**: [hrylabour.gov.in](https://hrylabour.gov.in/).
**District-level**: District Labour Commissioner (DLC), Panchkula.

**Officers** (verify current):
- Labour Commissioner, Haryana.
- Joint Labour Commissioner.
- DLC, Panchkula.
- Inspectors of Shops (territorial allocation in Panchkula).

### EPFO — Regional Office Faridabad / Panchkula sub-office

**Address (Faridabad)**: Bhavishya Nidhi Bhawan, Sector 16, Faridabad.
**Helpline**: 14470.
**Online**: [unifiedportal-emp.epfindia.gov.in](https://unifiedportal-emp.epfindia.gov.in/).

### ESIC — Regional Office Faridabad / Panchkula Branch Office

**Address (Faridabad)**: Panchdeep Bhawan, Sector 16A, Faridabad.
**Online**: [esic.in](https://www.esic.in/).

### Industrial Tribunal–cum–Labour Court — Ambala / Panchkula

Adjudication of industrial disputes under IR Code 2020.

### District Officer for POSH

Deputy Commissioner, Panchkula. Receives POSH IC annual report.

### HSIIDC — Panchkula Regional Office

**Address**: HSIIDC office in Panchkula (typically Sector 6 / IT Park area).
**Online**: [hsiidc.org.in](https://www.hsiidc.org.in/).
